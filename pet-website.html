History and Legend

Zen was kinapped as a young pup by a spirited and possibly intoxicated teen girl in rural Hermiston. Though the tiny chi-corgi-mutt missed her mama, and especially the ready access to milk, she grew to love her new family.

Zen's teen mom grew up, became a massage therapist with a busy work schedule, and made the dificult decision to rehome her. Once again, Zen was separated from her mother, but being a resilient and brave little dog, she attached to her new people with fierce loyalty.

Zen currently resides in Portland, OR. She is the CUTEST dog in the world.
